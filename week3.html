Research on Food Delivery

Earlier on this week, I compiled a list of questions to ask my participants for the questionnaire and interview.


Questionnaire Results
I received 25 responses for the questionnaire. Some of the things I observed in the answers were:
  • Majority are full-time students and working
  • Majority belong in the age category 15-24
  • 94% of our users are mid-incredibly busy
  • A lot of our users order food from Uber Eats, Deliveroo, Foodora or Menulog
  • However, majority of these participants don't frequently often order food
  • 76% of participants have had positive experience when it comes to food delivery
  • 72% of participants would be happy if an autonomous technology delivered their food


Interview Results
I interviewed 5 participants. Some useful UI and UX answers I've obtained:
  • 80% of students prefer validating on a mobile device than calling as a form of interaction in a food delivery app
  • Pain points: Interface is sometimes unclear and minimal error prevention
  • Highlights: Users enjoy seeing variety of food choices and interactive elements and visuals
