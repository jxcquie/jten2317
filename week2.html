Deciding on a concept

We received an additional team member in our group, Sandra/Jihyun. We filled Sandra on the details of what we did last week so we could all be on the same page. We talked to her about the concepts that we had in mind, and along with our tutor Hollie. We brainstormed more ideas however unfortunately it didn’t fit the criteria of interacting with an autonomous vehicle so we scrapped the ideas and decided if we wanted to follow through our first or second idea. We weighed the pros and cons of both ideas and finally decided that we should follow through with the second idea, which is an autonomous drone/robot that delivers people’s food efficiently, as the first concept would be extremely difficult to test with potential users as Sydney does not encounter major natural disasters.
