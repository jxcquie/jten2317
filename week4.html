Presentation day

Today was the day we presented individually in class. Needless to say, I could have improved the presentation by not having so much words in the slidedeck. I think I spoke too fast in the presentation because I felt like I wasn't going to finish on time, but in the end I finished about 30-40 seconds earlier.

In the end, I learned that the key to succeeding in presentations is that you need to engage your audience from the start to finish. And in order to do this, have less words and more visuals.
